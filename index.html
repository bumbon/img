About


If you want to make a gift for a birthday or to greet, then a good option would be picture embroidered with beads. The tree or flower woven of beads also will be a wonderful gift.

Art of manufacturing of beads reaches depths of ages. Making beaded decoration - one of the most mysterious and interesting kinds of folk art. Man by nature seeks to beauty. Since ancient times people have decorated their homes, clothes and household items. Many beautiful things you can create with the beads.
